Impact Award

Doc Academy

Doc Academy is an online platform providing award-winning documentary film for free use in the classroom.
BRITDOC believes that documentary film is one of the most effective tools in creating empathy and inspiring people to engage as active citizens at a local, national and international level. To change the way we see the world.
In 2012-13 we piloted the project in 18 schools. Since then we have taken it UK-wide.
